All Buddiez are equal, but some are more equal than others. That’s why we’ve established a trait rarity system to tell which of our students are likely destined for greatness.

There are 180+ total traits and these have been categorized into a tier based system (seen on the right). Traits are ranked from ‘common’ all the way through to ‘legendary’ and each rank has a corresponding % chance of being minted.
